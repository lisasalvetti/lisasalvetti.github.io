Lisa Salvetti

Ciao! My name is Lisa Salvetti and I am a second-year PhD student in experimental economics at the Center for Environmental Economics in Montpellier (CEE-M).

Before my PhD, I earned my MSc in Economics and Data Analysis from the University of Verona and worked as a research assistant at the GATE Lab, in Lyon.

My research focuses on how educational and behavioral interventions can foster sustainable, prosocial, and cooperative behaviors. My current projects involve field and lab experiments.
